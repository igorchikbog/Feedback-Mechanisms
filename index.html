Government Departments Feedback Mechanisms Analysis

Section 1

Summary of the Information Gathered

The information below is a summary of the information found on each chosen department's form as of June 9th 2023
that highlights similarities and differences with our feedback mechanism.

Department Feedback Form Similarities from SSC Form Differences from SSC form
Transport Canada Feedback Form
  • Easy to find
  • Easy to use
  • Very simple
  • Gives option for Anonymous feedback (but not required)
  • Smaller font
  • Not as many options for feedback
Employment and Social Development Canada Feedback Form
  • Easy to find (under the "Feedback" section of the Accessibility Plan page)
  • Gives option for Anonymous feedback (but not required)
  • Simple
  • You can submit video or letetypewriter feedback
Public Health Agency of Canada Feedback Form
  • Gives option for Anonymous feedback (but not required)
  • Gives Multiple options for different types of barriers
  • Hard to read
  • No option to contact through phone
Canadian Security Intelligence Service Feedback Form
  • Very simple form
  • The Contact Person field gives no place to leave your information
  • Gives more options to choose from when describing the area of your feedback
  • No word limit
Department of National Defence Feedback Form
  • No form, you can only submit through email
Canada Revenue Agency Feedback Form
  • Very straight forward
  • Easy to use
  • No option to submit complaints anonymously
  • Loads step by step, less to read at once.
  • If you want to be contacted, you need to provide a lot of information (SIN)
  • Hard to find
Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat Feedback Form
  • Large Font, easy to read
  • More option to Describe the area of your feedback.
  • No character limit.
Indigenous Services Canada Feedback Form
  • Very Similar to SSC's form
  • More options for feedback
  • No word limit
Public Services and Procurement Canada Feedback Form
  • Gives option for Anonymous feedback (but not required) and contact information
  • Gives option to contact through email
  • No option to contact through phone
  • Gives more options to choose from when describing the area of your feedback
  • No word limit
Canada Post Feedback Form
  • Very simple form
  • Option for contact through email or phone
  • Option to use live chat
  • Option to upload up to 5 attachments
  • ASL/Audio version of the Feedback Process available
  • Can only select 1 reason for contact
  • Very hard to find
  • No word limit
Canadian Border Services Agency Feedback Form
  • Feedback reason field
  • Comments field
  • Asks for evidence to support comments and/or complaint
  • Asks user to contact them by e-mail about their feedback
  • Asks the user for areas relevant to their feedback
  • Does not ask for any contact information except e-mail
Fisheries and Oceans Canada Feedback Form
  • Feedback reason field
  • Comments field
  • Asks user to describe themself (i.e. employee or member of the public)
  • Asks user to contact them by e-mail about their feedback
  • Asks user for region related to their feedback
  • Asks the user for areas relevant to their feedback
Canadian Air Transport Security Authority Feedback Form
  • Comments field
  • Telephone field (optional)
  • E-mail field (optinal)
  • Asks the user for the first and last name (optional)
  • Asks the user for their organization (optional)
  • Asks the user for their full address (optional)
  • Asks the user to please resubmit their feedback if they did not get an ackownledgement after 28 days.
  • reCAPTCHA
Administrative Tribunals Support Service of Canada Feedback Form
  • Feedback reason field
  • Comments field
  • E-mail field
  • Asks the user for areas relevant to their feedback
  • Asks user to contact them by e-mail about their feedback (optional)
  • Asks the user for their name (optional)
Canadian Heritage Feedback Form
  • Feedback reason field
  • Comments field
  • Asks the user for their e-mail (optional)
  • Asks the user about the type of accessibility barrier and/or area of the plan they have encountered
  • Asks user to contact them by e-mail about their feedback
  • Asks user if they want to subscribe to their newsletter to be consulted on Canadian Heritage accessibility plans, reports and other initiatives related to accessibility and disability inclusion
  • Asks the user for their name (optional)
Women and Gender Equality Canada Feedback Form
  • Comments field
  • Asks if the user wants to submit anonymous feedback
  • Asks the user what are the types of barriers to accessibility and/or areas of the plan they wish to comment on
  • Asks the user if they want a response to their feedback
Statistics Canada Feedback Form
  • Feedback reason field
  • Comments field
  • Asks the user for audio or visual feedback
Canada Council for the Arts Feedback Form
  • E-mail field (optional)
  • Feedback reason field
  • Comments field
  • Asks the user for their name (optional)
Library and Archives Canada Feedback Form
  • Feedback reason field
  • Comments field
  • E-mail address field (optional)
  • Asks the user for the type of accessibility barrier and/or area of the plan for which they would like to provide comments
  • Asks the user if they want to be contacted for a follow-up (optional)
  • Asks the user if they would like to be consulted on Library and Archives Canada accessibility plans, reports and other initiatives related to accessibility and disability inclusion
  • Ask the user for their name (optional)
Bank of Canada Feedback Form
  • Feedback reason field
  • Comments field
  • E-mail address field (optional)
  • Telephone field (optional)
  • Name field (optional)
  • reCAPTCHA field

    Section 3

    Takeaways to improve SSC's Feedback Mechanism

    • Implementing sign language (ASL and LSQ) for form instructions: Example - Canadian Heritage
    • Allow user to submit feedback in sign language (ASL and LSQ).
    • Allow user to provide video and audio feedback.
    • Allow user to indicate to which region their feedback is related.
    • Add a way for user to keep in touch with the team after submitting feedback. Could be a newsletter or us simply contacting them.
    • Allow user to show their interest for future consultations to feel like a part of the solution and to help SSC in their journey.
    • Include a reCAPTCHA to prevent spam and abuse.
    • Allow user to submit feedback via live virtual chat
    • Allow user to submit attachments related to their feedback
    • Remove Word Limit
